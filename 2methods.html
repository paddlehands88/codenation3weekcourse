Managing and executing such methodologies is challenging but if a project team can get this right, objectives will be achieved and surpassed.

Different projects will require different skill-sets and resources. This could be hard skills such as understanding different programming languages or how to send an email, but also includes soft skills such as critical thinking, creativity; interpersonal skills such as motivating others and leading by example.

Staple resources in modern businesses include computer terrminals (desktops, laptops, tablets, smartphones), printers and scanners etc and also associated software (Operating Systems, Word Processors, Spreadsheets, Email etc).

Digital project teams' choice of software will be dependent on projects aims and objectives. Programming teams will utilise Stack Overflow (stackoverflow.com) when troubleshooting programming related issues. The Mozilla Development Network (MDN) is a key resource for referencing different components of a programming language. Github.com is a website which allows programming teams to collaborate, and is used by 40 million developers worldwide.

While the choice of resources are important, it is imperative project teams and the individuals within them are mindful of the overall aim and objectives they are working toward to maintain individual and collective focus.
